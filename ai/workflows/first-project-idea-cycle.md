# Workflow: First Project Idea Cycle

## Goal
Create the first shortlist of strong project ideas for CEO review.

## Duration
3 working days.

## Day 1: Collect Problems
Business Analyst team collects problems from:
- CEO observations.
- Existing business network.
- Potential client industries.
- Repeated manual tasks in small businesses.
- Startup operations pain points.
- Opportunities where AI agents can reduce delivery cost.

Output:
- 20 raw problem statements.
- Target customer for each problem.

## Day 2: Convert Problems Into Ideas
For each promising problem, define:
- Project idea name.
- Target customer.
- Pain point.
- MVP solution.
- Revenue path.
- Required AI agents.
- Risks and assumptions.

Output:
- 10 project idea briefs.

## Day 3: Score and Shortlist
Score each idea using `ai/workflows/project-idea-discovery.md`.

Output:
- Top 3 ideas for CEO review.
- 3 ideas to research further.
- Ideas to park or reject.

## CEO Review
CEO decides for each top idea:
- Approve discovery.
- Build MVP.
- Park.
- Reject.
