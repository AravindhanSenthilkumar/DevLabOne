# Workflow: Project Idea Discovery

## Purpose
Help the Business Analyst team regularly generate, validate, score, and present strong project ideas for the CEO and leadership team.

## Owners
- Primary: Business Analyst.
- Supporting: CEO, Project Manager, Scrum Master, Solution Architect, UX Designer.

## Frequency
- Run light discovery daily when new ideas appear.
- Run a focused idea review weekly.
- Bring top ideas to the daily CEO meeting when a decision is needed.

## Sources of Ideas
- Client conversations.
- Founder observations.
- Support tickets and repeated customer pain.
- Manual internal work that can be automated.
- Competitor gaps.
- Industry trends.
- Compliance, reporting, or operational needs.
- Existing product limitations.
- AI-agent productivity opportunities.

## Discovery Steps
1. Capture the idea in simple language.
2. Identify the target customer and user.
3. Define the painful problem.
4. Confirm why the problem matters now.
5. Estimate business value and revenue path.
6. Check whether existing tools already solve it.
7. Estimate build complexity and required AI agents.
8. Identify risks, assumptions, and validation questions.
9. Score the idea.
10. Recommend one of: approve discovery, build MVP, park, reject.

## Scoring Criteria
Score each item from 1 to 5.

| Criteria | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Problem strength | How painful and frequent is the problem? |
| Target customer clarity | Do we know exactly who needs this? |
| Revenue potential | Can this become paid work or a product? |
| Speed to MVP | Can we build a useful first version quickly? |
| Strategic fit | Does it match company direction? |
| Competitive advantage | Can we do it better, faster, or cheaper? |
| Risk level | Lower risk gets a higher score. |

## Recommendation Guide
- 28 or above: strong candidate for CEO review.
- 22 to 27: research further.
- 15 to 21: park unless a client asks for it.
- Below 15: reject for now.

## Required Outputs
- Project idea brief.
- Score and recommendation.
- Required AI agents.
- Discovery questions.
- MVP scope.
- Estimated effort.
- Risks and assumptions.

## CEO Review Questions
- Is this aligned with company strategy?
- Can this create revenue or strong learning?
- Do we have the AI-agent resources to build it?
- What is the smallest useful MVP?
- What decision is needed today?
