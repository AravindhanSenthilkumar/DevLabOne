# Company

## Mission
Build reliable, affordable software products with the help of specialized AI agents, so small and growing businesses can move faster without sacrificing quality.

## Vision
Become a trusted AI-first software company where human leadership and AI agents work together to plan, build, test, secure, document, and improve products continuously.

## Values
- Customer clarity before code.
- Simple systems over clever systems.
- Security and privacy by default.
- Small releases, fast learning.
- Documentation as shared memory.
- Human accountability for AI-assisted work.

## Operating Principles
- Every feature must connect to a real user or business outcome.
- AI agents may draft, analyze, and implement, but humans approve product, security, financial, and legal decisions.
- Prefer reusable patterns, shared components, and documented decisions.
- Keep scope small enough to ship, measure, and improve.

## Company Policies
- Protect customer data and company secrets.
- Do not paste private credentials into AI tools.
- Use version control for all production code.
- Review every pull request before merge.
- Maintain a written decision log for architecture, security, product, and release choices.
