# FAQ

## Who approves AI-generated work?
A human owner approves product, code, security, release, and customer-impacting decisions.

## Can agents deploy to production?
Agents can prepare deployment steps and checks. A human must approve production deployment.

## Where are standards stored?
Standards are stored in `ai/standards`.

## Where are decisions stored?
Decisions are recorded in `ai/memory/decisions.md`.

## What happens when requirements are unclear?
The Business Analyst agent should clarify assumptions, write questions, and confirm acceptance criteria before implementation.
