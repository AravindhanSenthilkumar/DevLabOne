# Business Domain

## Domain Summary
The business focuses on software delivery, AI automation, customer operations, and digital product development.

## Common Business Capabilities
- User management.
- Customer onboarding.
- Role-based access.
- Reporting and dashboards.
- Workflow automation.
- Notifications.
- Audit history.

## Success Metrics
- Time to deliver features.
- Customer activation.
- System uptime.
- Defect rate.
- Support volume.
- Revenue retention.
